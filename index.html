About

I am an M.Sc. student in System Modeling and Data Analytics at the University of Tehran, where I am advised by Prof. Reza Tavakkoli-Moghaddam.

My thesis, “Towards autonomous supply chains for perishable products: Learning-based decision-making for coordination, competition, and resilience,” is expected to be completed in December 2026. My research sits at the intersection of operations research and machine learning, with a focus on dynamic pricing, inventory control, and resilient coordination in logistics and supply chain systems — often through reinforcement learning and convex optimization.
